Caleb Stripes

I have found writing code to be such an astounding parallel to many of my other interests. I find it compelling seeking and inventing solutions to all kinds of issues, this creative outlet is precious. In climbing I have to find just the right way to move and position my body to progress, in code the placement of each variable and function satisfies the same means when solving software challenges. Each time I ride my mountain bike I can push myself through features better and faster than the last time, each time I research a solution I find at least three other things I can do with code. Every time I stand watch and collaborate with a crew to harness the forces of air to move thousands of pounds of solid material, I see new ways of combining the parts of a ship. Just as each time I sit down to code jam I find new ways to combine the tools for specialized solutions. In all these things I have failed frequently, yet I can't stop, and won't stop. For I am an adventurer, and no adventure worth having is without failure.
